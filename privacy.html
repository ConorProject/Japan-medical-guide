How we protect your medical and personal information

üîí Your Privacy is Our Priority

Bottom line: All your personal, medical, and travel information stays completely private on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your sensitive data.

What Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal information: Names, addresses, passport numbers, email addresses
  • Medical information: Medication names, dosages, quantities, medical conditions
  • Travel information: Arrival dates, hotel bookings, flight details
  • Generated documents: Permit applications, doctor letters, or any forms you create
  • User accounts: No registration, no passwords, no profiles

Information We Do Collect (Anonymous Usage Only):

  • Basic website analytics: Page views, general location (country level), browser type
  • No tracking across sessions: We can't identify individual users or link visits together

What Happens When You Use Our Tools:

  1. Medication Search: Searches our local database (no data sent to servers)
  2. Permit Wizard: All personal information stored only in browser memory
  3. Document Generation: Forms created locally using JavaScript templates
  4. Download: Documents generated in your browser, no server involvement

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Lives:

  • Your browser only: Personal information never leaves your device
  • Session storage: Automatically deleted when you close the browser tab
  • No databases: We don't have servers storing user information
  • No cloud storage: Documents aren't saved to any online service

What We Can't Access:

  • Your completed forms or personal information
  • Your generated documents
  • Your medication lists or medical details
  • Your travel plans or personal data

Third-Party Services

Services We Use:

  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting (static files only, no data collection)
  • Google Fonts: Typography (may log font requests)
  • Basic Analytics: Anonymous page view statistics only

Services We Don't Use:

  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Advertising networks
  • Data collection services
  • Email marketing platforms
  • User behavior tracking

Your Rights and Control

You Have Complete Control:

  • Clear data anytime: Close browser tab to delete all session data
  • Use incognito/private mode: For extra privacy protection
  • Block JavaScript: Site won't work, but guarantees no local processing
  • Choose what to share: Only you decide what to send to Japanese authorities

International Users

Since all processing happens locally in your browser, your data never crosses international borders through our systems. The only international data transfer is the initial download of our website files (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and the public medication database.

Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for adults applying for medication import permits. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 18. Since we don't collect personal information from anyone, this isn't a practical concern.

Questions About Privacy?

This privacy policy reflects our commitment to protecting your sensitive medical information. If you have questions about how our privacy protection works, you can:

  • Review our open-source code (all processing is transparent)
  • Use browser developer tools to verify no data transmission
  • Contact us through our GitHub repository for technical questions

Policy Updates

If we ever change our privacy practices, we'll update this page and note the changes clearly. However, our core commitment will remain: your personal and medical information stays private on your device.
