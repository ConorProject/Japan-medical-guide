üö´ Completely Prohibited Medications

These medications CANNOT enter Japan under any circumstances, even with permission:

Amphetamine-Based ADHD Medications

Adderall, Dexedrine - Contain amphetamine which is classified as "Stimulants" under Japanese law

Real Case: US citizen arrested and detained 18 days for bringing Adderall to Japan, despite having valid prescription

Pseudoephedrine Cold Medicines

Sudafed, Actifed, DayQuil/NyQuil varieties - Use Japanese alternatives like Pabron Gold A

Cannabis & CBD Products

All cannabis derivatives, CBD oil, medical marijuana - Zero tolerance policy in Japan

Certain Opioid Combinations

Codeine combinations over Japanese limits - Check specific thresholds
